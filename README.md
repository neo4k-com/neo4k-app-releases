# NEO4K

Watch movies, TV shows, and live channels on your Android device.

## Download

Grab the latest `.apk` from the [Releases](../../releases) page and install it on your phone or tablet.

> Android may ask permission to install apps from outside the Play Store the first time. Tap **Allow** when prompted.

## Install

1. Open the downloaded `.apk` file from your **Downloads** folder.
2. Tap **Install**.
3. Once installed, open NEO4K and sign in with your account.

When a new version is released, download the new `.apk` and install it the same way — your favorites and settings stay.

## What you can do

- **Browse movies and shows** with posters, overviews, cast, director, country, runtime, and trailers.
- **Watch episodes** of TV shows season by season.
- **Tune in to live channels.**
- **Search** across movies and shows by title.
- **Save favorites** — long-press any poster or channel to add it to your favorites.
- **Pick your look** — Light, Dark, or follow your system theme.
- **Choose your language** — English or العربية.

## Requirements

- Android phone or tablet.
- An active internet connection.
- A NEO4K account to sign in.

## Need help?

If something isn't working:

1. Make sure you have the latest version from the Releases page.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. Try closing and reopening the app.

For anything else, use **Contact support** inside the app (Settings → Support).
